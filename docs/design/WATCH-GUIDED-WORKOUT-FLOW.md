# CORE PRINCIPLE

> **The AI programs the workout. The watch guides you through it. You just tap DONE.**

The workout sequence is **pre-defined** before you start. The watch knows:
- What segment comes next
- How long/how many reps
- Your personal targets
- Everything is integrated

**User's only job: Execute and tap DONE when finished.**

# SUMMARY

## The Experience

1. **AI generates workout** with full sequence
2. **Watch receives workout** with all segments pre-defined
3. **User starts workout** on watch
4. **Watch guides through each segment** - shows what's current, what's next
5. **User taps DONE** when segment complete
6. **Watch auto-advances** to next segment
7. **Repeat until workout complete**
8. **Full analysis** on phone with isolated run/station data

## Key Principles

- **No decision-making during workout** - just execute and tap
- **Watch knows everything** - sequence, targets, personal bests
- **Manual segment completion** - user controls the pace
- **Rich feedback** - pace guidance, HR zones, haptics
- **Perfect data isolation** - every run segment knows its context
