## üìù BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

### Core Principles:

**1. Be Data-Transparent**
```
‚ùå "This workout will help you improve."
‚úÖ "Your Station 3 time is 12% slower than average. This workout targets that."
```

**Why:** HYROX athletes track everything. Show them the data.

---

**2. Explain the "Why"**
```
‚ùå "Do 5√ó5 sled pushes."
‚úÖ "5√ó5 sled pushes to build explosive power. Your station times show this is your limiter."
```

**Why:** Process goals (understanding actions) drive motivation better than outcome goals.

---

**3. Acknowledge Discomfort, Don't Sugarcoat**
```
‚ùå "You can do this!"
‚úÖ "HYROX is supposed to hurt. This workout prepares you for that."
```

**Why:** Athletes embrace discomfort. Respect their toughness, don't patronize.

---

**4. Personalize, Don't Generalize**
```
‚ùå "Great workout for HYROX training!"
‚úÖ "Based on YOUR pace data, this workout is designed for YOU."
```

**Why:** 73% engagement increase with AI personalization (Freeletics data).

---

**5. Focus on Process, Not Just Outcomes**
```
‚ùå "Get faster times!"
‚úÖ "Improve your weakest station first. Speed follows."
```

**Why:** Process goals = higher adherence and long-term commitment (research-proven).

---

**6. Use Active, Performance Verbs**
```
Action verbs: Adapt, Target, Build, Fix, Push, Train, Measure, Improve
Avoid: Try, Hope, Wish, Feel, Believe
```

**Why:** Action-oriented language drives conversions (fitness messaging research).
